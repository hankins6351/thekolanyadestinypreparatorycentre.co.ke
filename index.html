About us

The kolanya Destiny Preparatory Centre is a private, mixed, Co-educational, non-denominational school geared towards the provision of high quality education both 8-4-4 and CBC carriculum.It has a kindergarten and primary school

Founded on 14th January 2017, Our Director Mr. Titus Echuku envisioned a performing school. The Kolanya Destiny Preparatory Centre is founded on a very strong and firm christian principles which are futher enhanced by the school.

ENROLMENT 

The school calendar begins in January and ends in November. New student join the school in the beginning of our academic year. 2021 intake is on progress from ECDE (EYE) to grade 5.

Facilities

Since the inception, The Kolanya Destiny Preparatory Centre emphasizes on on the highest standards in interlectual growth in the development of social, moral, spiritual & academic values in preparing pupils for Secondary, Tertiary education and ultimately the feauture. It encourages pupils and the staff to learn and work in an atmostphere of tolerance and in awareness of the richness of our diversity.
